What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers try to trick you into giving sensitive information or performing actions that compromise security. Common types include:

  • Email Phishing: Fake emails pretending to be from trusted sources
  • Spear Phishing: Targeted attacks using personal information
  • Whaling: Attacks targeting high-level executives
  • Smishing/Vishing: Phishing via SMS or phone calls
